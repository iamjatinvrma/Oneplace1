Tic Tac Toe

I developed a classic Tic Tac Toe game using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. This game allows two players to take turns marking their symbols (X or O) on a 3x3 grid. The objective is to be the first player to align three of their symbols vertically, horizontally, or diagonally.
